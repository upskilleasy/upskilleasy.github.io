Create Freestyle project job

  1. Dashboard page

  1. Create a job / New Item

2.1 Enter job name

2.2 Select Freestyle project

2.3 Click on OK button

  1. Configuration page

3.1 On General, provide description: “Sample freestyle project”

  1. Select Build Steps

        Select “Execute Shell” as our Jenkins is running on a Linux machine.

On selecting “Excecute shell” option you will be able to see a text area, where you can provide some command.

Let’s provide basic linux command: echo <Some message> as below

  1. Click on “Save” button

  1. You will be able to see following page:

  1. Click on “Dashboard”

Here you will be able to see the list of jobs created, along with some status.

If you hover the mouse over the job name, then you will see a drop-down with different options appear below the job name. Be careful, if you directly click on the job name, the job detail page will be opened instead of drop-down.

  1. Execute the job by clicking the Play button available on the right side of the job name.

On click of the Play button, the job will be scheduled and executed.

Observe following:

  1. The status under “S” column is updated.
  2. The Dates of Last Success / Failure
  3. Last Duration

  1. Click on Job name (Free_style_job1) to check the details

 

  1. On the left menu the last option shows the no. of times the job ran. Click on #1 (which is job execution no.) You will be able to see the job status.

  1. Now click on “Console Output” to see the job execution output.

Observe that the “echo” command and it’s output can be seen here.

  1. You can execute the same job multiple times by clicking on “Play” button or “Build Now” from the left menu.
